About Me

Hello everyone! My name is Jane and I am a professional photographer. I specialize in wedding, portrait and family photography, but I also like to experiment with different genres and styles. Photography is not just a job for me, but a true love since childhood.

I believe that true photography is more than just a pretty picture. It has the ability to convey emotions, moods and feelings. That's why I always try to create an atmosphere of comfort and trust during filming, so that you can be yourself and feel natural in front of the lens. I invite you to a photo session to preserve the bright moments of your life for you!
